We are an interactive voice response system platform company that give reproductive health information in preferred local languages to adolescents and young people in Uganda. We believe in giving affordable real-time health information to our customers. Currently, we specialize at easing last-mile access to contraceptive information and services. We started this because most people fear talking about sex yet it is everywhere in our communities and culture. For this single reason, contraceptives use is highly acceptable in private but widely criticized in public. Infopic bridges this privacy gap by giving customers information on the use of available contraceptives, possible side effects and directions to the nearest equipped health facility. Our platform is accessible offline through a mobile number or online through our Mobile Application. Lately, we are looking forward to using "Nearest Neighbors" to further ease the distribution process.
